Which Epcot rides should I use Genie Plus on?

Genie Plus is a new service offered at Disney’s Epcot that allows guests to access shorter wait times for select attractions. While it may be tempting to use Genie Plus on every ride, it’s important to choose wisely in order to make the most of your experience. Here are a few Epcot rides where using Genie Plus can enhance your visit:

1. Test Track

Test Track is one of the most popular attractions at Epcot, known for its thrilling high-speed ride experience. By using Genie Plus, you can skip the regular standby line and enjoy a faster entry into the ride. This is particularly helpful during peak times when wait times can be longer.

2. Soarin’ Around the World

Soarin’ Around the World takes guests on a breathtaking flight over iconic landmarks and landscapes. With Genie Plus, you can bypass the regular line and spend less time waiting, allowing you to experience more of Epcot in a day.

3. Frozen Ever After

Frozen Ever After is a must-do attraction for fans of the beloved film, Frozen. It takes guests on a magical boat ride through the kingdom of Arendelle. By using Genie Plus, you can minimize your wait time and immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Elsa, Anna, and Olaf.

4. Spaceship Earth

Spaceship Earth is a classic Epcot ride that takes you on a journey through the history of communication. While wait times for this attraction are usually manageable, using Genie Plus can allow you to skip any potential lines and make the most of your time at Epcot.

5. The Seas with Nemo & Friends

For underwater adventure lovers, The Seas with Nemo & Friends is a must-visit attraction. By utilizing Genie Plus, you can reduce your wait time and dive into the colorful and charming world of Nemo, Dory, and their friends.

These are just a few examples of Epcot rides where using Genie Plus can be beneficial. However, it’s important to note that the availability of Genie Plus and the specific attractions included may vary. It’s always a good idea to check the official Disney website or consult with a Cast Member for the most up-to-date information.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does Genie Plus cost at Epcot?

Genie Plus is available for a daily fee of $15 per person at Epcot. This fee allows you to access shorter wait times for select attractions, subject to availability.

2. Can I purchase Genie Plus in advance?

Yes, you can purchase Genie Plus in advance through the My Disney Experience app or at designated kiosks throughout the park. It is recommended to purchase it early to secure your preferred attractions.

3. Are all Epcot rides included in Genie Plus?

Not all Epcot rides are included in Genie Plus. Some attractions, such as Mission: SPACE and Test Track, have a separate individual Lightning Lane entrance, which requires an additional fee.

4. Can I use Genie Plus for multiple parks in one day?

Yes, Genie Plus is valid for use at multiple parks in one day. This allows you to enjoy the benefits of shorter wait times at various Disney parks.

5. Can I use Genie Plus for popular character meet and greets?

No, Genie Plus does not include access to character meet and greets. However, there may be separate virtual queues or FastPass+ options available for certain character experiences.

6. Are there any height or age restrictions for Genie Plus?

No, Genie Plus is available to all guests, regardless of age or height. However, individual attractions may still have their own height restrictions for safety reasons.

7. Can I get a refund if I decide not to use Genie Plus?

Refunds for Genie Plus are subject to Disney’s refund policy. It is advisable to review the terms and conditions before making a purchase.

8. Can I book Genie Plus for my whole vacation in advance?

No, Genie Plus can only be purchased on a daily basis. You will need to purchase it each day you wish to use it.

9. Is Genie Plus available during special events or holidays?

Yes, Genie Plus is typically available during special events and holidays. However, it’s important to check the official Disney website for any updates or modifications to the service during these times.

10. Can I use Genie Plus on all days of my visit?

Yes, you can choose to use Genie Plus on all days of your visit or only on select days, depending on your preferences and schedule.

11. Are there any other benefits to using Genie Plus?

In addition to shorter wait times for select attractions, Genie Plus also offers benefits such as access to Disney PhotoPass service and audio experiences exclusive to the Genie app.

12. Can I make changes to my Genie Plus selections?

Yes, you can modify your Genie Plus selections throughout the day using the My Disney Experience app or at designated kiosks. This allows you to make adjustments based on your changing plans and preferences.

Remember, when using Genie Plus at Epcot, it’s important to prioritize the attractions that are most important to you and plan your day accordingly. With the right strategy, Genie Plus can help you make the most of your time and create unforgettable memories at Epcot.
