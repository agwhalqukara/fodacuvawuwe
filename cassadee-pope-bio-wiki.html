Who is Cassadee Pope?

Cassadee Pope is a singer, songwriter as well as a musician. Her real name is Cassadee Blake Pope and belongs to American society. She was the lead vocalist of the rock band Hey Monday, with whom she released one studio album and three EPs in 2011.

Furthermore, in 2012 she had released Cassadee Pope EP as well as embarked on a solo career. Additionally, her debut solo country album, Frame by Frame was released and had reached the top 10 Billboard 200 charting in 2013.

Cassadee Pope: childhood, education, and family

Blake Pope was born on 28th August 1989 in Florida, U.S. She is the daughter of father Lenny Pope and mother Lori Pope as well as has a sibling named Ashley Pope. There is not any information regarding her childhood.

Cassadee Pope had addressed Wellington High School in Wellington, Florida, and had formed the band Blake with Mike Gentile.

Cassadee Pope: Professional and career history

Coming towards the experienced part of her life, she started her career in 2008. This year, she had appeared in Fall Out Boy’s video for “America’s Suitehearts as well as wrote two songs, and had co-written the other nine songs. The next year, she had provided guest vocals for The Cab’s remix version of their song “Take My Hand” which appeared on Fall Out Boy’s mixtape and appeared in the music video. In 2010, her first EP Beneath It All was to be released, and had to promote the album the group toured the US and played Warped Tour.  She had also released The Christmas EP in 2011 and also took an indefinite hiatus on good terms.

At the beginning of 2012, she embarked on her first solo acoustic tour across the east and west coasts of the United States.  She had released her debut self-titled EP which contained four songs written by herself. She had auditioned for the singing competition The Voice. And had also performed that night with Grammy award-winning group Train at the NIVEA Kiss Stage in Times Square in New York City. All these activities were done in the same year.

The next year, Cassadee Pope signed with the country music label Republic Nashville. She had toured with the artists Tim McGraw and Dierks Bentley in 2014. In 2015, she released the lead single, “I Am Invincible” from her upcoming second studio album. She had also sung the Star Spangled Banner the United States national anthem in 2016.

Cassadee Pope: awards and achievements
