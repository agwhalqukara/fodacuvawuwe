Tony Khan Reveals MJF &amp; Adam Coles Insane Merch Sales As A Tag Team

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 16, 2024

The Better Than You, Bay Bay tag team has been working very well not only for Adam Cole and MJF but for All Elite Wrestling as well.

All Elite Wrestling owner Tony Khan recently sat down with Mike Johnson of PWInsider. When asked about the Adam Cole and MJF tag team, Khan revealed that the team as a duo has sold more merchandise in the past couple of months than any other act this year.

“There’s also a lot of excitement about what’s happening around the AEW World Champion MJF and his tag team partner Adam Cole,” Tony Khan said. They’ve had a great run right now on TV across Dynamite and Collision as a tag team, Better Than You, Bay-Bay.

“It’s also been very successful commercial merchandise really in addition to the great TV ratings; I have to say that Adam Cole and MJF as a team have sold more merchandise just in these past couple months as a team than any other act in AEW all year.”
